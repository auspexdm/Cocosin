About Coco's Inn Resort

Get ready to live, for unlimited living

Coco's Inn Resort is a picturesque coastal retreat nestled along the shoreline of Alibaug, boasting panoramic views of the sparkling ocean waters. It's conveniently located within easy reach of popular tourist attractions, yet secluded enough to provide a peaceful escape from the hustle and bustle of city life.

The resort offers a variety of accommodation options to suit every traveler's preference, including spacious oceanfront rooms with balcony. Each room is elegantly furnished and equipped with modern amenities, ensuring a comfortable stay.

Guests can embark on nature walks, enjoy bonfires and poolside dining, play indoor and outdoor games with family, or simply relax with a book on the sandy shores.

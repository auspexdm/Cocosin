Home · Dining

Experience Coco's Inn — cultured in hospitality, relaxation & luxury

Nestled in the serene coastal beauty of Alibag, our resort is not just a place to stay, but a destination for culinary exploration. Dining is an integral part of the travel experience — a diverse range of flavours reflecting the rich cultural tapestry of the region.

Our Kitchens

From beachside romance to poolside family meals

Our dining venues cater to every taste — from traditional local cuisine to international favourites. Whether you're indulging in a romantic dinner by the beach, a casual family meal by the pool, or a gourmet feast at our main restaurant, each dish is crafted with care and passion.

Our chefs use the freshest ingredients, many sourced locally, so every bite is a testament to quality and authenticity.

Signature Cuisine

Kokani Delights

We take pride in offering an authentic taste of the Konkan coast. Kokani cuisine is known for its bold flavours, fresh ingredients and unique combinations — one of India's most beloved regional cuisines, mastered by our chefs.

Kokani Fish Curry

A spicy, tangy curry made with the freshest catch, cooked in a flavourful coconut gravy with traditional spices.

Chicken Sukka

A dry chicken dish with grated coconut, aromatic spices and a hint of kokum for a distinctive taste.

Sol Kadhi

A refreshing, cooling drink of coconut milk and kokum — perfect after a spicy meal.

Bombil Fry

Crispy fried Bombay duck, marinated in spices — crunchy texture, burst of flavour.

Our Story

Born of Sea, Sand
& Coastal Calm

Nestled in the coconut groves of Barshive village, just 4 km from Kashid's pristine shores, Coco's Inn was built as an act of devotion — to the Konkan coastline and to the belief that true comfort lives in simplicity.

"A retreat for those who seek the sea's quiet, the shore's warmth, and a home away from everything ordinary."

Every room opens to the sound of the wind. Every meal carries the flavour of the coast. We did not build a resort — we uncovered a sanctuary that was always here.

Nature

The sea, the monsoon soil, the coconut groves — they are not backdrop. They are host. Every room breathes with the land around it.

Warmth

We treat every guest as family. Our hospitality is shaped by generations of Konkan tradition and a sincere desire to make you feel at home.

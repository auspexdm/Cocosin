Signature Cuisine

Kokani Delights

We take pride in offering an authentic taste of the Konkan coast. Kokani cuisine is known for its bold flavours, fresh ingredients and unique combinations — one of India's most beloved regional cuisines, mastered by our chefs.

Kokani Fish Curry

A spicy, tangy curry made with the freshest catch, cooked in a flavourful coconut gravy with traditional spices.

Chicken Sukka

A dry chicken dish with grated coconut, aromatic spices and a hint of kokum for a distinctive taste.

Sol Kadhi

A refreshing, cooling drink of coconut milk and kokum — perfect after a spicy meal.

Bombil Fry

Crispy fried Bombay duck, marinated in spices — crunchy texture, burst of flavour.

Local ingredients: the coastal region of Alibag provides an abundance of fresh seafood, coconuts and tropical fruits — all integral to Kokani cuisine. Our dishes are a celebration of these local flavours, offering a true taste of the region.

Home · Things To Do

More closer to paradise

Nestled in the serene coastal village of Barshiv, Coco's Inn Resort offers more than just luxury and relaxation — it's a gateway to unforgettable experiences. From the sun-kissed shores of Kashid Beach to the historical wonders of Murud-Janjira Fort, immerse yourself in the perfect blend of adventure, culture and nature.

Experience the magic of sun, sand and sea

Explore Kashid Beach — just minutes away

The perfect blend of serenity and adventure. Relax on the soft sands, take long walks along the shore and soak in the sea breeze — a must-visit gem for every guest.

Step into the past — an unconquered legacy

Historical charm at Murud-Janjira Fort

A short drive away, this island fort is famous for remaining unconquered despite several invasion attempts. The scenic boat ride adds a layer of adventure to your historical exploration.

Panoramic views of the Arabian Sea

Korli Fort

Perched on the cliffs, this lesser-known gem offers sweeping sea views. Its ruins speak of an ancient past, while the peaceful surroundings make it a favourite for history lovers and trekking enthusiasts alike.

A beacon on the horizon

Korli Lighthouse

Standing tall near Korli Fort, this iconic structure has guided sailors for centuries — now a vantage point for sweeping coastline views, perfect for photographers and breeze-seekers.

Spiritual solace on a hill

Birla Mandir

A beautiful marble temple dedicated to Lord Ganesh, perched with breathtaking sea views — known for its peaceful ambiance, immaculate architecture and landscaped gardens.

History wrapped in nature

Revdanda Fort

Once a Portuguese stronghold, now in peaceful ruins overgrown with lush vegetation — an intriguing blend of history and nature with picturesque ocean views and hidden corners to explore.

Reconnect with nature

Phansad Wildlife Sanctuary

Immerse yourself in rich biodiversity — a refuge for countless species of flora and fauna across dense forests. A paradise for birdwatchers, nature lovers and wildlife photographers, whether hiking the trails or simply soaking in the calm.

Home · How to Reach

Getting here is part of the journey

Coco's Inn Resort sits in the coastal village of Barshiv, just 2.6 km from Kashid Beach on the Alibaug–Murud coastal road. Reach us by road or by ferry — and let Google Maps guide you door to door.

Get Directions from My Location Open in Google Maps
Ways to Reach

By road or by sea

All distances and travel times below are approximate — please verify current ferry timings before travelling, especially during monsoon season.

Approx. 130–140 km · 3.5–4 hrs from Mumbai

By Road from Mumbai

Take the Mumbai–Goa Highway (NH-66) via Panvel and Wadkhal, then follow the Alibaug–Revdanda–Kashid coastal road to Barshiv. The final stretch along the coast is one of the most scenic drives in Maharashtra.

Route from Mumbai
Approx. 170–180 km · 4–4.5 hrs from Pune

By Road from Pune

Drive via the Mumbai–Pune Expressway to Khopoli and onward through Pen–Alibaug, or take the lush Tamhini Ghat route via Mangaon and Roha for a greener drive down to the coast.

Route from Pune
Ferry ~1 hr + scenic 1.5–2 hr drive

By M2M Ferry via Mandwa (the fun way)

Take the M2M Ferries Ro-Pax from Bhaucha Dhakka (Ferry Wharf, Mumbai) to Mandwa Jetty — about an hour across the harbour, and you can carry your car, bike or cycle onboard. From Mandwa, drive down the coast via Alibaug and Revdanda to the resort. Speedboats from the Gateway of India are an alternative. Schedules vary by season, so book ahead.

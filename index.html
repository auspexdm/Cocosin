Why Choose Coco's Inn?

Coastal comfort with a touch of luxury near Kashid Beach

At Coco's Inn, we believe great hospitality doesn't have to come at a high price. Just 2.6 km from the breathtaking Kashid Beach, our serene seaside retreat offers travelers a smart alternative to crowded, overpriced resorts — without sacrificing style, comfort, or charm.

Wake up to refreshing sea views, unwind by the pool, or stroll through our expansive green lawn as the ocean breeze drifts by. Thoughtfully designed rooms blend modern elegance with the warmth of home, creating the perfect space to relax after a day of sun and sand.

Whether you're here for work, a weekend escape, or a peaceful family getaway, Coco's Inn invites you to experience the beauty of Kashid in comfort and style.
